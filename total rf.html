What is a serum?

A serum is applied to your skin after cleansing but before moisturising creams with the intent of introducing powerful ingradients into the skin. Serums are particulerly suited to this task because skin with a high concentration of key ingredients. This makes them a great tool for targeting specific skincare concern.

Why are face serums important for anti-aging?

hydration, deeper lines, wrincles, dullness + lack of firmness. Adding at least one serum to your regimen based on your primary targeted concern ampliflies
